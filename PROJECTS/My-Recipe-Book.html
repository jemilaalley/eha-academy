Goat Pepper Soup

Ingredients

  • 1.2kg goat meat, cleaned
  • 2-4 hot peppers
  • 1 small onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 thumb sized fresh ginger
  • 1 tablespoon peppersoup mix
  • Handful chopped scent leaf
  • Salt to taste

Directions

  1. Combine hot pepper, onions, garlic and ginger in a blender. Blend till coarse.
  2. In a medium pot, combine goat meat with pepper blend and salt. Simmer on low heat for 30 minutes.
  3. Add 6 cups of water and peppersoup spice to the pre-cooked meat and stir.
  4. Simmer for another 20 minutes or until meat is tender. Garnish with scent leaf and remove from heat.

Stewed Beans and Plantain

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups cleaned brown or black eyed beans
  • 3 large bell peppers
  • 2 large onions
  • 2 tablespoon palm oil
  • 2 stock cubes
  • Salt to taste

Directions

  1. Combine hot pepper, onions, garlic and ginger in a blender. Blend till coarse.
  2. In a large pot, add in beans and water. Leave to cook until soft.
  3. In another pot, add in 1 tablespoon of oil, add in some chopped onions and fry until fragrant.
  4. Add in stew base, crayfish, seasoning, salt, beans and water.
  5. Simmer on low heat until plantain is cooked through.
